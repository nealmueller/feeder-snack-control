Privacy Policy

Feeder Snack Control is an unofficial macOS utility that sends a snack command to supported feeder hardware using the credentials you provide.

What the app stores

  • Your feeder service login email.
  • Your feeder service password in the macOS Keychain.
  • Your selected feeder identifier and basic local app settings.
  • Authentication tokens returned by the feeder service, stored locally on your Mac to avoid repeated sign-in.

What the app sends

  • Your login credentials are sent to the feeder service only to authenticate your device.
  • The app sends feeder control requests only when you ask it to perform an action.

What the developer collects

The developer does not operate a separate analytics, advertising, or customer data collection service for this app.

Third parties

The app communicates with the feeder vendor's services to authenticate and send feeder commands. Use of those services is also subject to the vendor’s own terms and privacy practices.

Data retention and deletion

You can use the in-app Clear Local Data action or remove the app from your Mac at any time. Deleting the app or its local data does not delete your account with the feeder vendor. To delete that account, use the vendor’s own account management and support channels.

Contact
